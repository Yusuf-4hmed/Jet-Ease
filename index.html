Where to get this infomation
To use the tools on Jet Ease, you'll need the following details for your flight:
  • Departure City: This is the city where your flight starts. You can usually find this information on your flight itinerary or booking confirmation under the "From" section.
  • Departure Time: This is the time when your flight takes off. Look for the scheduled departure time on your flight ticket or itinerary.
  • Arrival City: This is the city where your flight lands. You'll find this in the "To" section of your flight details.
  • Arrival Time: This is the time when your flight is expected to land. It will be listed on your itinerary or booking confirmation.
If you're on a connecting flight, you’ll need to enter the departure city and time from your first flight, and the arrival city and time from your last flight to ensure the correct calculations.
